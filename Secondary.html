Detailed Description

Cornelia Musat is a 40 years-old psychologist that dedicated her late life to psychology domain. Sure, she has a decade of experience under her belt, specializing in CBT(Cognitive behavioural therapy) and mindfulness. But Cornelia also sees the potential technology holds for improving mental healthcare accessibility. That's why she jumped at the opportunity to work with a new app designed to be a smart companion for mental well-being.

Every day, Cornelia starts with some meditation and tells her clients how great it is. She checks out how people are using the app, gives them tips based on what she sees, and sometimes chats with them through video calls on the app.

She thinks it's important for people to have online friends who support them. Cornelia joins online meetings with the app's team to make sure everything is going well.

Her big goal is to help people feel better about themselves and make it okay for them to ask for help when they need it. With her experience using therapy websites and apps, Cornelia believes this new app can make a real difference in how we take care of our mental health.

Cornelia is a big fan of the app's ability to give people the help they need, when they need it. She's excited to see how the app can change people's lives for the better.
